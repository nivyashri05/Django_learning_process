Why Choose First IPO

Convenience To Employers & Candidates

It's a online browser based face to face interview application which is built for the corporate to complete their HR & Technical virtually.

Time and Cost Savings

Since you have more information about the candidate at the time of pre-screening, it would significantly reduce the time your team would spend on one-on-one telephonic calls to assess a candidate.

Avoid Fake Interviews

Since the entire process of Candidate walking into the office till the time he is onboard, is recorded and documented. Hence it facilitates recruiters to avoid fake candidates by keeping a proof of their hiring process and verifying the same at any given point of time. 

Audit Trails

It also enables audit trails since all the recruitment processes and steps are documented and recorded with reports.
